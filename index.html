Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Encouraging new ideas and technologies to drive economic growth and development.

Sustainability

Focusing on environmentally friendly practices and resource management for long-term prosperity

Infrastructure

Building and improving essential facilities like transportation, energy, and communication networks

Empowerment

Enabling individuals and communities to take control of their future through education, skills, and opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to
enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure
a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support
economic growth and global competitiveness.

Poverty is not an accident. Like slavery and apartheid, it is man-made and can be removed by the actions of human beings. We can create a world where poverty is not the destiny of anyone,and every individual has theopportunity to live a life of dignity. The systems we build and the policies we implement should serve humanity, not enslave it. Empowering people, especially women,through microcredit, education, and opportunities can break the cycle of poverty and bring about lasting change for future generations.

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
